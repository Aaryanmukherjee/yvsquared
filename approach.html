Welcome to

Your Voice Your Vote

Your Voice Your Vote is a non-partisan, non-profit political advocacy organization with one simple goal: to increase voter turnout amongst suppressed populations. For too long, the voices of low-income, minority populations have been silenced and squashed — we aim to change that.

PROGRAM
& PROJECTS

To combat voter suppression, the founders have formulated a strategized, data-driven solution which incorporates education as well as facilitation. Every month, and sometimes more during election season, we hold voting booths at community centers to make the voting process easier to navigate; we hold seminars to educate people on the importance of the vote. We recently joined efforts with Michelle Obama’s organization When We All Vote, expect bigger and better projects soon!
